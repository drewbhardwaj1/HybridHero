OFFICE PARKING SOFTWARE

A parking system for hybrid workplaces

When planning, remove uncertainties by securing a parking place. To reserve a parking space, HybridHero’s parking management software records employees' check-in and check-out times. You may also designate and distribute parking spaces to certain teams/employees.

  • Schedule parking from your smartphone
  • Make sure your parking in the right space
  • Locate spaces and people from the workplace maps

PARKING OCCUPANCY MANAGEMENT

Make managing parking areas seamless

To minimise congestion, get a consolidated view of the parking spot. Consolidated parking occupancy data allows you to keep track of the number of bookings made, free space available, and booked time slots.

  • Easily locate a space from the workplace map
  • Reserve a room from the calendar or your smartphone
  • Clear filtering to match rooms to user needs
  • Monitor how rooms are used to best manage your resources

Automate your workplace parking

CLEAR COMMUNICATION

Keep colleagues informed

Employees are immediately alerted when a parking spot is booked, including specifics such as bay number and booking time. Any adjustments you make to your reservation will instantly free up the current space for someone else to utilise.

  • Better understand room utilisation trends
  • Reconfigure under-utilised spaces to better serve your people
  • Forecast office needs with leading workspace analytics

parking ANALYTICS

Download real-time parking spot utilisation data to make better use of the available space. You may obtain comprehensive information on everything from peak use time to available slots, waiting time to cancelled bookings.

  • Forecast office needs with leading parking analytics
